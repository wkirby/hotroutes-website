Fact Sheet

Game Description:
Hot Routes is a Virtual Reality game where you step into the pocket and test your abilities as a quarterback. With a variety of different planned gameplay modes — from quick mini games to a full 7-on-7 style passing game — Hot Routes offers a variety of ways to play.
Key Features:
  • Virtual Reality
  • Football
  • Four Different Modes
    • Hot Routes: The receiver's running their route. Can you complete the pass?
    • Time Attack: Time is counting down. Throw the ball through the hoops before the clock runs out.
    • Trick Shot: Make each throw count. Move through a preset series of challenges, but don't miss.
    • 7 on 7: A full 7-on-7 styling passing game played against an AI defense. Read the defense and take your team to the endzone.
  • Global leaderboards
  • Steam achievements
Early Access Demo:
Available upon request. Contact press@hotroutesgame.com
Current Development Status:
Hot Routes is currently in a pre-alpha state. The game contains placeholder art assets and only the Hot Routes and Time Attack modes are playable.
